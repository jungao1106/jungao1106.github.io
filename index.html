About Me

I am a Ph.D. student (2025–2029) at Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, advised by Xiaoxue Ren (ZJU100 Young Professor). Currently, I am a research intern in the Multimodal Team of Soul AI (So Aura Talent Program). Before that, I obtained my master's degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Soochow University in 2025, advised by Prof. Ziqiang Cao. My research interests include VLMs, code intelligence, and human‑centric AI.
